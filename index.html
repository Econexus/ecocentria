About The Econexus Journey

A growing number people are living and working to reduce the environmental impact of our societies. Households and businesses alike often feel isolated in their sustainability efforts and have to figure out the best way for themselves. Econexus brings ecocentrics together to support each other and to raise up the emerging sustainable economy. It's a new form of a very old concept. Econexus aims to create ecocommons: shared knowledge, skills and resources for the community to steward for the next generation.


This is our first step: make face-to-face and virtual platforms for people to connect. We hope you enjoy sharing this unfolding story.
