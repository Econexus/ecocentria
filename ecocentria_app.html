Get Inspired Swipe & Select

Ecocentria offers you a stream of ideas. These are positive actions that could help you create a sustainable society. You can accept or reject each idea with a swipe action.

Get Organised Prioritise & Plan

The ideas you accept appear on your shortlist. Here, you can organise them into categories by colour (or discard them). The categories can mean what you like: priorities, timespan or person responsible, perhaps. Then you can tick them off as you master them.

Get Going Join In & Find Out

The real power of Ecocentria lies in its active community of users. When you explore an idea, you can tell everyone what works - add a resource to the idea. You can rate the ideas to help other people prioritise. And you can offer ideas of your own.
